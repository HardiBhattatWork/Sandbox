The New York Artist Showcase

Sep 14, 2007 - Jan 14, 2009

This exhibit features the work of the NYC members of the webcomics collective ACT-I-VATE which debuted in 2006 on the blog LiveJournal.

The online community was conceived as an experimental cyber studio, where artists can collaborate, showcase their work and engage in dialogue with their audience. Since its beginning with 8 member cartoonists, the collective has grown to 32 members who are creating comics for free online for readers worldwide. http://community.livejournal.com/act_i_vate/

Act-I-Vators featured in the exhibit are: Mike Cavallaro, Kevin Colden, Nikki Cook, Michel Fiffe, Simon Fraser, Ulises Farinas, Dan Goldman, Dean Haspiel, Tim Hamilton, Jason Little, Josh Neufeld, Leland Purvis, Ryan Roman, Jennifer Tong, and Dave Wallin.
