She Draws Comics: 100 Years of America's Women Cartoonists

May 20 - Nov 6, 2007

The show celebrates a century of the greatest female practitioners of comic and cartoon art that America has ever seen, from the Nell Brinkley Girl of the turn of the century to the wartime adventures of Miss Fury and Brenda Starr, to today's cutting-edge manga and graphic novels.

With over 50 artists participating and over 100 artworks shown, this is the largest retrospective of women’s work in comic and cartoon art ever mounted. The show is curated by the foremost authority on the subject, scholar and artist Trina Robbins.
