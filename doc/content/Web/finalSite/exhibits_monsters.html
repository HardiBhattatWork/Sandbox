Monsters. Things That Go Bump…

May 12, 2008 - Jan 28, 2009

MoCCA’s “Things That Go Bump…” is an all-ages exploration of the ways iconic monsters have been represented in artwork from comics, cartoons and animation. It is a piercing gaze into the ways in which the human experience has been reflected in the malformed shapes of traditional monster archetypes. The artwork on display features zombies, demons, vampires, ghosts, and witches.

Favorites from the past as well as new creatures will be on display, including Dracula and Frankenstein, Hellboy, Elvira, Sabrina the Teenage Witch, Casper the Friendly Ghost, and many more.
