About Us

Board of Directors

Matthew C. Murray (President and Executive Director)
Matthew received his BFA in film and television production from NYU's Tisch School of the Arts and is pursuing a certificate in not-for-profit arts management. An avid fan and collector of animation artwork, Matthew was the lead curator for MoCCA's popular "Saturday Morning" exhibit and is recognized as a leading "Smurfologist."

Allan Dorison (Chief Operating Officer)
Allan is the conference coordinator for Credit Suisse Financial Bank. He has been instrumental to MoCCA's Art Fest and has served as facilities manager for the museum since 2005. Allan is a lifelong reader of comic books and is a collector of comic art.

Bill Roundy (Secretary)
Bill Roundy is a freelance writer and editor. He has been a MoCCA volunteer since 2005, and helped to curate MoCCA's exhibits: "Infinite Canvas: The Art of Webcomics" and "Things that Go Bump...". Bill draws the webcomic journal The Amazing Adventures of Bill, and has contributed to the gay romance comic Young Bottoms in Love.

Sandra Schechter (Vice President)
Sandy graduated from Mount Ida College in Newton, MA with a degree in liberal arts/journalism. She joined direct sales at Marvel and is currently permissions manager at Bedford/St. Martin's. From her interest in adventure and serial cartoon art Sandy started a collection and library of original political cartoons, comic strips, and illustrations.
