Become a Member

MoCCA's success depends on the commitment of its members to help fund its programs, exhibits, and events. Become a member now and support and enjoy comic and cartoon art in all its dimensions, and receive the benefits of MoCCA Membership.

Core Benefits include:

  • Membership card and MoCCA pin
  • Free admission to the MoCCA Gallery
  • Access to the MoCCA Library
  • 10% discount at the MoCCA Gift Shop
  • Reduced admission to MoCCA events
  • Invitations to Members-only events
  • Reduced table fees for the MoCCA Art Festival

Donate or Volunteer
You can support our mission with your donations or time.

With your financial contributions you will invest in our future and help us grow and thrive as the major museum of comic and cartoon art.

You can assist in special events, promote membership, work on marketing and fundraising, or donate artwork and other comics and cartoon related items to be sold at fundraising events.

Because we are a nonprofit organization, all donations to MoCCA are tax-deductible.

If you would like to donate or volunteer, please call (215) 426-3311 or e-mail us at info@moccany.org or volunteers@moccany.org
