About Us

History

Founded in 2001, MoCCA is the only museum and art gallery in the eastern United States dedicated to the collection, preservation, study, education, and display of comic and cartoon art. Every genre of the art is represented: animation, anime, cartoons, comic books, comic strips, gag cartoons, illustration, editorial cartoons, caricature, graphic novels, and computer-generated art.

In 2003 MoCCA opened a small art gallery on Union Square with a debut exhibit on single panel gag art. The following year the museum moved to a larger facility in Soho at 594 Broadway. Since its opening MoCCA has mounted many exhibits emphasizing the universality of the medium in all its forms. In addition, the museum offers workshops, lectures, and education programs for schoolchildren. Since 2002 MoCCA organizes an annual Art Festival which is widely recognized as the most important promoter of independent and alternative comic art.
