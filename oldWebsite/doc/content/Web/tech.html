Security features
SSL tied to the e-commerce section of the site

Email newsletter distribution
Brings the latest comics and news to our visitors

Shopping cart
Allows visitors to browse and shop from our museum store online

News/press release area
Offers press releases on the latest exhibits, also includes positive reviews of the museum in the news

No

No, all code will be rewritten in valid CSS and XHTML.
