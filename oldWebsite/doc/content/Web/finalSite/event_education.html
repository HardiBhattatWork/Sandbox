Post-Bang Symposium
“Post-Bang: Comics Ten Minutes After the Big Bang!” is an all-day symposium on the growing cultural significance of comics. The event, free and open to the public, is being held in association with this year's MoCCA Art Festival and will take place on June 6, 11am - 9:30pm, at the Cantor Film Center at NYU, 36 East 8th Street (at University Place).

For more information, visit
http://www.nyih.as.nyu.edu/object/nyih.postbangcomics.html

Education Programs
MoCCA offers arts education programs to New York City's schoolchildren. In these programs, given at the museum and at schools, comic book artists teach children the steps involved in the making of a comic book, from story idea and scripting, to character design, drawing, coloring, and publishing.

For more information, call 215.254.3511 or e-mail us at education@moccany.org

Workshops
MoCCA offers workshops for people of all ages. In our The Art of Making Comics and DIY Comix & Zine Making workshops you learn to make your own comix from story ideas from sketches & thumbnails, through to finished product.

For more information, call 212.254.3511
