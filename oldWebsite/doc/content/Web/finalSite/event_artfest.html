Art Festival

The Museum of Comic and Cartoon Art is proud to announce the MoCCA Art Festival 2008, to be held June 7th-8th, 2008

Location:
The Puck Building, 293 Lafayette and MoCCA Gallery, 594 Broadway, Suite 401.

Hours:
11:00am - 6:00pm

Admission:
$10 each day / $15 weekend pass

Information:
212.254.3511
info@mocca.org

This annual event is widely recognized as the most important promoter of independent and alternative comic art. The program for this year’s Festival features a rich mix of animators, cartoonists, graphic artists, and writers. Special guests include Jessica Abel, Rebecca Donner, David Hajdu, David Heatley, Chip Kidd, Alex Robinson, Frank Santoro, and Brian Wood.

Saturday’s program opens with author Blake Bell talking about his new Steve Ditko biography, and closes with Dan Nadel in conversation with Chris Forgues (“CF”). Sunday’s program opens with an illustrated history of radical cartooning, by social movement cartoonist Nick Thorkelson, and closes with a screening of new animated shorts from Scandinavia.
