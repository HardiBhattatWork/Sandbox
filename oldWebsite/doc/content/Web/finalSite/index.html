Welcome to the

Museum of Comic and Cartoon Art!

MoCCA's mission is to promote the understanding and appreciation of comic and cartoon art as well as to detail and discuss the artistic, cultural, and historical impact of what is the world's most popular art form.

To that end, the museum hosts exhibits and events, conducts educational programs, and serves as a resource for those conducting scholarly or professional research on comic and cartoon art.
