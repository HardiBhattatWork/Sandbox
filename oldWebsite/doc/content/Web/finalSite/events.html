Events

Overview

MoCCA organizes an annual Art Festival featuring the best in independent and alternative comic art. This year's festival will be held on June 7th and 8th from 11am to 6pm, in the Puck Building at 293 Lafayette and the MoCCA Gallery at 594 Broadway.

Each year, a number of award ceremonies recognize outstanding achievements in the comic book industry and cartoon arts. Among the ceremonies held at MoCCA are: the Art Festival Award presented to creative figures whose work has elevated comic and cartoon art; the Lulu Award which honors noteworthy women in the industry; and the Harvey Award, one of the industry's oldest and most respected awards.

To increase public awareness and appreciation of comic art, MoCCA offers education programs for schoolchildren, workshops for people of all ages, and lectures and symposia for all interested in the cultural and historical significance of comic art.
