Online Exhibits

Now Then

What did professional comic artists draw like when they were 12 years old, you ask? The Museum of Comic and Cartoon Art is pleased to answer the question with NOW THEN, our second online exhibit.

Dave Bamundo had the idea for an exhibit that featured comic artists' current work, compared to something they had done as a child. At the same time, I was working on an exhibit focused on the favorites among professional comic artists. NOW THEN is a combination of these two ideas.

Robert Zimmerman
