Infinite Canvas: The Art of Webcomics

Sep 14, 2007 - Jan 14, 2008

The exhibit explores three aspects of online comics: the unique format and design of webcomics, their appeal to niche audiences, and the transitions between web and print comics.

Webcomics are free of the space constraints and editorial censorship to which printed comics are often subjected and provide an outlet for a great diversity of creators and audiences.

Infinite Canvas incorporates original artwork, prints of finished art, and digital displays. Featured are Penny Arcade, PhD, Sluggy Freelance, User Friendly, Diesel Sweeties, Mom’s Cancer, Finder, Supernatural Law, Questionable Content, Something Positive, Scary Go Round, Achewood, Narbonic, Goats, Checkerboard Nightmare and many others.
